Workflow

How the dealer buying workflow works.

The goal is not just to touch more Teslas. The goal is to touch better ones, faster, with clearer reasons to buy or move on.

01

Discovery

We start with your store, market, margins, and how aggressive you want to be in the lane.

02

Define what moves

We narrow the Tesla trims, years, price bands, and buy patterns that make sense for your rooftop.

03

Owner connects the Tesla

When it fits the workflow, the owner can go through the owner connect flow so the opportunity is easier to verify and understand.

04

Benchmark and structure the buy

We look at the live market, use Carvana or CarMax offers when useful, and compare the opportunity against your own tools and appetite.

05

Move or pass

If it is a fit, we move the owner toward the next step, including in-store handoff when location makes sense. If not, you reject faster and cleaner.

FAQ

Dealer questions answered fast.

What does dealer work cost?

Dealer discovery is $750 flat. Pilot and ongoing dealer sourcing are quoted after discovery based on store needs, lane, volume, and tool access.

Can you work with our tools?

Yes. If your store uses Accu-Trade, vAuto, or another appraisal or buying stack, I can work inside that process or run independently and hand back a Tesla-specific recommendation.

How do you screen private-party Teslas?

We start with the owner, the car, and the story. When it fits the workflow, I use the owner connect and OutCheck flow so the opportunity is easier to verify before your store spends more time on it.

Are you the dealer or a broker?

No. This is independent Tesla-only acquisition support. Your store stays in control of the appraisal, structure, title process, and buy decision.

Do you only work locally?

No. I work remotely and can help dealers nationwide, schedule permitting. If the owner is close to your dealership, the next step can move in-store.
